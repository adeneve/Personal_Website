About me

I am 23 years old, born and raised in California. I spent most of my early childhood and adolescence in Ventura County. I went to Westlake Highschool, where I gained interest in engineering and played for the school's doubles tennis team. After highschool, I left Southern California to study at the University of California, Santa Cruz. UCSC sits on a large plot of land close to the shoreline and is split up into multiple colleges. At UCSC I was part of Crown College, the college focused on the sciences and engineering. In June of 2018, I graduated with my Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science.

As of now, I am living in Thousand Oaks, California.

Some hobbies of mine- I like to hike, build and scrap computers, and cook.
